Privacy Policy

TeamDrip Finance App | Effective Date: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nielsen Partners Group, LLC ("Company") handles information in connection with the TeamDrip Finance App ("App").

1. Data Collected

The App accesses QuickBooks Online financial data including transactions, accounts, and company information solely for the purpose of internal financial reporting and management.

2. Use of Data

Data accessed through the App is used exclusively for internal business operations of Nielsen Partners Group, LLC and TeamDrip. Data is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on Company-owned systems. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond the Intuit QuickBooks API.

4. Security

Company implements reasonable technical safeguards to protect data accessed through the App.

5. QuickBooks Data

This App connects to Intuit QuickBooks Online via the Intuit Developer API. Use of QuickBooks data is governed by Intuit's terms of service and privacy policy.
