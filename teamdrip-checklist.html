Product Photo Standard
Use these specs for ALL product photos going forward — consistency is everything
✅ Recommended Standard — All TeamDrip Product Photos
Ratio 1:1 (Square)
Dimensions 2048 × 2048 px minimum
File Format JPG (smaller file size, faster load)
Background Consistent — pure white (#FFFFFF) or brand neutral
Max File Size Under 1MB per image
Color Profile sRGB (standard web)
Product Placement Centered, with ~5-10% padding on each side
💡 Already have photos that aren't square? Don't crop the product — add white space on the sides to pad them into a square. You can batch-do this in Canva (free) or Lightroom. Just open the image, set canvas to 1:1, and center the product. Takes about 30 seconds per photo.
📱 Shooting new photos? Set your iPhone to shoot square in the Camera app (swipe the format selector), or shoot wider and crop in post. Make sure lighting is consistent across all products — same background, same light setup every time. One great afternoon of shooting can get you all the photos you need.
